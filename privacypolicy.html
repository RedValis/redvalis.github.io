Privacy Policy

For the Valisave browser extension

Valisave does not collect, transmit, or sell personal data.

All information handled by the extension is explicitly provided by the user and is stored locally on the user’s device using Chrome’s local storage. The extension does not send any data to external servers and does not use analytics, tracking, or advertising technologies.

The extension accesses website content only when the user explicitly interacts with supported pages in order to save citation metadata. This information is used solely to perform the extension’s core functionality.

No personal information, browsing history, or identifying data is collected, shared, or sold to third parties.

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you may contact the developer.
